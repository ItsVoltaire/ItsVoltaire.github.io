The Land's Edge

You walk straight ahead and eventually you find that the land ends in that direction. It's not halted by water or anything; it's just a giant drop off from this land. Gazing down the edge, you can see clouds and a land far below with specks that look like cars. With this, you realize this is some sort of sky island of sorts.

Where do you go now?
